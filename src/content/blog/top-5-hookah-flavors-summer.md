# Top 5 Hookah Flavors to Try This Summer

Summer is the perfect time to relax with a refreshing hookah session. Here are our top 5 flavor recommendations for the season.

## 1. Watermelon Mint

A classic summer combination. The sweetness of watermelon pairs perfectly with the cooling sensation of mint.

## 2. Peach Iced Tea

Refreshing and sweet, this flavor is like sipping a cold glass of iced tea on a hot day.

## 3. Citrus Mix

A zesty blend of lemon, lime, and orange. Perfect for those who prefer a tangy profile.

## 4. Blue Melon

A unique mix of blueberry and melon. It's sweet, fruity, and incredibly smooth.

## 5. Tropical Punch

Transport yourself to a tropical island with this blend of pineapple, mango, and passionfruit.
